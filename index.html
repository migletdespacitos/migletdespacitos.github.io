About Me

Hello! I am Miguel, a Year 4 undergraduate at the National University of Singapore, majoring in Data Science and Analytics. My academic journey has equipped me with a strong foundation in machine learning, statistical analysis, and the practical application of data science techniques to solve complex problems. Beyond technical mastery, I am passionate about leveraging data-driven insights to shape better policies, guide responsible decision-making, and contribute meaningfully to society. I believe that data science should serve the greater good by transforming information into understanding, and understanding into action that improves lives and drives positive change. With a background that blends mathematical rigor, computational skill, and a strong sense of purpose, I aspire to apply data science thoughtfully to create solutions that are impactful, inclusive, and enduring.
